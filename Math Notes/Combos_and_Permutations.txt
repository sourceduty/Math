Combos & Permutations

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Single: 1 item
Double: 2 items
Triple: 3 items
Quadruple: 4 items
Quintuple: 5 items
Sextuple: 6 items
Septuple: 7 items
Octuple: 8 items
Nontuple: 9 items
Dectuple: 10 items

-------------------------------------

Permutations (Order Matters):

P(n, r) = n! / (n - r)!

Example: P(5, 3) = 5 × 4 × 3 = 60

Combinations (Order Does Not Matter):

C(n, r) = n! / [r!(n - r)!]

Example: C(5, 3) = P(5, 3) ÷ 3! = 60 ÷ (3 × 2 × 1) = 10

Definitions:

n: Total number of items in the set
r: Number of items selected at a time
!: Factorial (e.g., 5! = 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 = 120)

P(n, r): Permutations (order matters)
C(n, r): Combinations (order does not matter)
